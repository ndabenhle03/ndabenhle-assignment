About Greens&Things

Fresh, Organic, and Sustainable – Nourishing Mpumalanga’s Community. Discover our journey to bring the freshest produce from local farms to your table, fostering health and sustainability.

Discover Our Story

Our Story

From Humble Beginnings

Greens&Things was founded in the heart of Mpumalanga with a vision to connect the University of Mpumalanga community and surrounding areas with the freshest organic produce. Our passionate team of agriculturalists works tirelessly to bridge the gap between local farms and your table.

A Fresh Future

We partner with local farmers dedicated to sustainable, chemical-free farming. Every fruit and vegetable is hand-picked at peak ripeness, ensuring unparalleled freshness. At Greens&Things, we’re cultivating a healthier, more connected community.

Our Mission

Nourishing Communities

Our mission is to provide wholesome, organic food while championing sustainable agriculture. We empower local farmers, promote eco-friendly practices, and make fresh produce accessible to all, fostering a greener, healthier South Africa.

Building Trust

Transparency is at our core. We’re committed to showing you where your food comes from, ensuring trust and quality in every order. Choose Greens&Things to support a movement for sustainability and community well-being.

Our Values

Fresh & Quality

We deliver produce that meets the highest standards of taste and nutrition, hand-picked at its peak.

Sustainability

Our eco-friendly farming methods protect the planet for future generations.

Community Focus

We build strong relationships with local farmers and serve our customers with integrity.

Health & Well-being

We promote a healthy lifestyle through accessible, wholesome food.

Delivery Details

We offer fast and reliable delivery services directly to your location within the University of Mpumalanga campus and surrounding areas. Our delivery schedule is:

  • Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Saturday: 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM
  • Sunday: Closed

A small delivery fee may apply depending on your distance from our collection point. You will see the exact fee at checkout.

Our delivery team handles your fresh produce with utmost care to ensure it arrives in perfect condition.

Physical Collection Details

You can choose to collect your order directly from our designated collection point at the University of Mpumalanga. This option is free of charge.

Collection Point: UMP Agriculture Department, Main Campus, Mbombela

Collection Hours:

  • Monday - Friday: 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM
  • Saturday: 9:30 AM - 1:30 PM
  • Sunday: Closed

Please bring your order confirmation email (digital or printed) for a smooth collection process. Orders are usually ready for collection within 2 hours of payment confirmation.

Easy Payments

We provide multiple secure and convenient payment options to make your shopping experience hassle-free:

  • Credit/Debit Card: We accept Visa and Mastercard. All transactions are encrypted and secure.
  • EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer): Bank transfer details will be provided upon checkout if you select this option.
  • Mobile Payments: Coming Soon!

Your payment information is always protected, and we do not store sensitive card details on our servers.

Enjoy a seamless and secure checkout process with Greens&Things!
